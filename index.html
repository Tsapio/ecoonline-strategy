Connecting With Our Audience

A successful strategy must address the distinct pain points and content needs of two critical personas: the practitioner and the executive.

"Samantha, the Safety Manager"

The Hands-On Practitioner

Overworked and under pressure, Samantha seeks practical, actionable solutions to her immediate problems. She needs content that saves her time and makes her job easier.

●
Pain Point: Administrative overload and endless paperwork.
●
Pain Point: Difficulty with employee engagement in safety.
●
Content Need: Short, "how-to" videos and practical guides.

"David, the Director"

The Executive Budget Holder

Focused on ROI, risk mitigation, and efficiency, David needs to see the strategic business case. He engages with high-level, authoritative content.

●
Pain Point: Navigating complex regulatory and compliance risks.
●
Pain Point: Lack of a unified, real-time view of organizational risk.
●
Content Need: Expert interviews, trend analysis, and ROI case studies.

The Three-Pillar Content Strategy

Our new mission: To become the most trusted educational resource in the EHS & ESG industry. This will be executed through three strategic content pillars.

Pillar 1: Educate & Empower (Top of Funnel)

Objective: Attract a broad audience of EHS professionals by solving their most pressing problems. The goal is to build brand awareness and establish foundational trust by being genuinely helpful.

Example Video Concepts:

  • "A 5-Step Guide to Flawless OSHA Recordkeeping"
  • "3 Ways to Make Your Toolbox Talks More Engaging"
  • "What is the Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD)? Explained in 5 Minutes"
  • "Digitizing Your Safety Audits to Save 10 Hours a Week"

Pillar 2: Demonstrate & Differentiate (Middle of Funnel)

Objective: Nurture the attracted audience by showing how EcoOnline's platform uniquely solves their problems. The focus is on tangible value and outcomes, not just features.

Example Video Concepts:

  • "Customer Spotlight: How Valvoline Centralized Chemical Safety"
  • "Scenario Demo: From Spreadsheet Chaos to a Streamlined Audit in 3 Minutes"
  • "EcoOnline vs. Manual Tracking: A Side-by-Side Comparison for Incident Reporting"
  • "A Deep Dive: Managing Safety Data Sheets (SDS) with Chemical Manager"

Pillar 3: Lead & Inspire (C-Suite Focus)

Objective: Establish EcoOnline as a high-level thought leader shaping the future of the industry. This content is designed to build brand prestige and engage executive decision-makers.

Example Video Concepts:

  • "The ROI of Safety: A C-Suite Roundtable Discussion"
  • "ESG as a Growth Driver: A Conversation with CEO Tom Goodmanson"
  • "The 'Situation Room' Series: Tackling the Biggest ESG Challenges with Industry Leaders"
  • "Navigating the New Climate Disclosure Landscape: An Expert Panel"

The Dual-Platform Strategy

We will leverage two distinct video platforms to maximize both brand reach and lead generation, engaging prospects at every stage of their journey.

YouTube: The Discovery Engine

The primary goal is to maximize organic reach and build a loyal subscriber base. Success hinges on rigorous SEO, including:

  • Targeting high-intent keywords EHS managers search for.
  • Designing compelling, high-contrast thumbnails to maximize clicks.
  • Optimizing for audience retention by delivering value in the first 15 seconds.

Wistia/Vidyard: The Conversion Engine

This platform will host mid-to-bottom-funnel content on the website to convert traffic into qualified leads using tools like:

  • In-video lead capture forms to gate high-value demos.
  • Seamless CRM integration to pass lead data to the sales team.
  • Advanced analytics like video heatmaps to provide sales intelligence.

Distribution & Promotion Playbook

Creating great content is the first step. A proactive, multi-channel distribution strategy is essential to ensure it reaches the right audience and drives action.

LinkedIn Promotion

As the primary social channel, we will share native video clips to promote C-suite focused content and drive traffic to full videos.

Email Marketing

New videos will be promoted to segmented email lists, ensuring practitioners receive tactical "how-to" content and executives receive strategic interviews.

Sales Enablement

The sales team will be equipped with a library of customer stories and demos to share in their outreach, with real-time notifications when a prospect watches.

Paid Amplification

A dedicated budget will be used to promote top-performing content via targeted YouTube and LinkedIn ads to reach new, relevant audiences.
